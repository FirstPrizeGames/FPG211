Operating principles

Keep the system useful when it matters.

The site is arranged around a few durable rules: reduce noise, keep trust boundaries clear, and make every route earn its place.

  1. 01
    Serve first

    Put clarity and usefulness ahead of decoration.

  2. 02
    Stay calm

    Organize information so visitors do not have to fight the interface.

  3. 03
    Tell the truth

    Keep privacy, payment, and product claims plain and easy to verify.

  4. 04
    Build small systems

    Separate long information into dedicated pages instead of crowding the homepage.

  5. 05
    Leave room to improve

    Make the site easy to revise as the profile, tools, and support options evolve.

Privacy. Payments. Trust.

The site keeps preferences local, explains public policies in dedicated pages, and sends checkout activity to Stripe instead of handling card details itself.

  • Local preferences Theme, language, and layout settings stay in the browser.
  • Stripe checkout Payment links open Stripe-hosted checkout pages.
  • Visitor control Settings include payment blocking and comfort controls.
